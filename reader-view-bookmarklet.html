Usage Instructions

For Regular Web Pages:

  1. Visit any article page
  2. Click the bookmarklet
  3. Article will be saved automatically

For Safari Reader View:

  1. Open an article and switch to Reader View
  2. Click the bookmarklet
  3. If prompted, paste the original article URL
  4. Article will be saved with cleaned content

Features

  • ✅ Reader View Detection: Automatically detects Safari Reader View
  • ✅ URL Recovery: Finds original article URL from metadata
  • ✅ Clean Content: Uses Reader View's cleaned content when available
  • ✅ Dual Protocol: Tries both HTTPS and HTTP APIs
  • ✅ Error Handling: Clear feedback on success/failure
  • ✅ Tagging: Marks reader view articles for easy identification
💡 Pro Tip: For best results with Reader View, make sure you're on the original article page before switching to Reader View, so the URL metadata is preserved.
